Key findings: 5 consensus genes appear in ≥3 tissues and ≥2 methods: MUP22 (4 tissues), NPAS2 (3 tissues, circadian), PER2 (3 tissues, circadian), GM24497 (3 tissues), THRSP (3 tissues, lipid metabolism). Cross-method agreement varies: skin/lung show highest Jaccard overlap (0.25), while thymus is lowest (0.005). Cell 2020 validation shows limited overlap (1-2/66 genes), reflecting that SHAP importance captures predictive features (not necessarily fold-change magnitude), and spaceflight signatures are tissue-specific.
