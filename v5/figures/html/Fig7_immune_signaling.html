mMCP-counter deconvolution, OmniPath L-R pairs, and decoupler-py TF activity across 8 tissues

*FDR < 0.05   **FDR < 0.01   †Single-mission (5-fold CV)

Druggability of spaceflight-affected genes per tissue
Key findings: Skin shows the strongest immune remodeling (6/14 cell types FDR<0.05), followed by kidney and thymus (2 each). Cross-organ L-R analysis identifies 62 active ligand-receptor pairs linking tissues during spaceflight. TF activity reveals widespread transcriptional reprogramming: thymus (240 TFs), skin (241), kidney (177), and liver (105) show significant FLT vs GC differences. Thymus and kidney are the most druggable tissues (24.8% and 21.5% of spaceflight-affected genes targeted by known drugs).
